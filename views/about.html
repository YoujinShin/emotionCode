THE CONCEPT

We wanted a way to show people not only how important understanding our emotions are but specifically, how they can be used to inspire our creativity levels. The Decoder is the first stab at developing an algorithm that helps people crack their emotion code. We hope you play with it and let us know what you think and how it can be evolved and improved.
